| ABOUT US

OUR LEGACY

Founded in 1923 by four ambitious brothers - Harry, Albert, Sam, and Jack Warner - Warner Bros. has grown to become one of the most iconic entertainment companies in the world. From the golden age of Hollywood to the digital era, we’ve been at the forefront of innovation, storytelling, and entertainment.

OUR STUDIOS

With state-of-the-art facilities in Burbank, California, and a global presence spanning across continents, Warner Bros. Studios is where magic comes to life. Our soundstages have echoed with the laughter of comedies, the drama of epics, and the thrills of blockbusters.

OUR BRANDS

Our diverse portfolio includes iconic brands such as DC Comics, HBO, and Cartoon Network. Whether it’s superheroes saving the day, epic fantasy worlds, or animated adventures, we offer something for everyone.

OUR COMMITMENT

At Warner Bros., we’re not just in the entertainment business; we’re in the business of creating experiences that bring people together. We’re committed to pushing boundaries, fostering creativity, and delivering entertainment that resonates with audiences of all ages.
